About me

Engineer focused on shipping practical AI systems, and the infrastructure that keeps them standing.

Hi, I'm Shoaib.

I'm a Full-Stack Developer and Data Scientist with a BE in Computer Science. I work across the whole stack: designing ML pipelines, building backend services, and shipping the interfaces that make them useful.

My recent focus: RAG systems with hybrid retrieval, LLM-powered automation, supervised fine-tuning and reinforcement-learning feedback workflows, computer vision, and real-time WebSocket APIs running on AWS data infrastructure. Day to day that means FastAPI, Flask, NestJS, Node, React, and Next.js, with PostgreSQL, MongoDB, and vector stores like Qdrant and Pinecone underneath.

I care about systems that hold up in production: clear interfaces, good observability, and evaluation loops that make the next iteration better than the last.
